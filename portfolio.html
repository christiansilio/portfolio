About Me

I am a 21 year old marketing senior at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte, seeking a full time position post-graduation in any field relating to marketing, marketing analytics, or within the sports industry.

My opportunities at the Belk College of Business helped me land an internship at Amwins for the Summer of 2023 in the Charlotte, NC office. I started working for the communications team within the marketing department, where I was tasked with duties consisting of internal and external marketing, social media, data analytics, and Excel database development.

I recently got promoted to be the marketing intern of a new division within the company: Amwins Digital. Within this department, I am responsible for creating a variety of graphics and email campaigns to promote new company technologies throughout Q3 and Q4.

Additionally, I am currently interning with the UNC Charlotte Women's Basketball team to help them with data analytics and scouting reports for the 2023-2024 NCAA season. I work directly with the assistant coaches, providing detailed reports to help strategize, game-plan, and develop new coaching techniques to help them return to March Madness brackets all around the country.

In the Spring of 2023, I participated in the UNCC x Carolina Hurricanes Analytics challenge, where I met with the team's business analytics department and they assigned my team three different tasks to present to them at PNC arena. A month later, I competed in the ESPN x USPS Direct Effect Challenge, where I worked with fellow UNCC marketing students to create an integrated marketing pitch for representatives from the two prestigious companies.

All of my experiences within the fields of marketing and analytics have helped me develop a diverse blend of creative qualitative and quantitative skills that I apply to my everyday life.
